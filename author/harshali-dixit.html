Harshali Dixit

writer

Harshali Dixit is a poet from Pune India. She is a first year medical student pursuing physiotherapy with a passion for poetry and writing in general. Her parents being writers themselves are her soul support in this field and also the reason of her being here today.

She has had a keen interest in writing from a very early age. She has notably won many poetry and essay competitions.

She has written articles for magzines in Hindi, English as well as Marathi.

“The moment you start trusting your decisions, rather than what others think, that moment changes your script forever”

She started work as a poet by writing for a client and since then there has been no U turn.

Life is a canvas for her and she had already decided to paint it with her own colours free of any kind of judgement.
