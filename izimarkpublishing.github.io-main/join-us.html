Hello Present & Future Authors,

Thank you for taking the time to read about our organization. As you have already observed, we are a “new school” online publishing company that invests in Authors world-wide. We provide services including but not limited to: topic generation, management, editing, design, publishing, advertising and marketing.

We employ analytical tools, market research and other marketing strategies to choose the works we will publish and how to present them. We take pride in such aspects as cover design and online marketing methods to increase the visibility of our Authors’ work. By our taking on this role, Authors can focus on and enjoy the process of writing. 

We pay upfront fees and costs, because you are not just investing in us, we are investing in you. We take these steps so that your book will be seen by more eyes, realizing its full potential.

We strive to bring our Authors success. We love working with new Authors and sharing innovative ways to grow and to succeed.

All the best,
IziMarkPublishing
