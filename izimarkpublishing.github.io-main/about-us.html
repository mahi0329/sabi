About Us

If you are currently a writer and work as a freelancer, we offer you the opportunity to expand your knowledge and potential by building your brand as an Author. We give full credit to our Authors. We also support and promote empowerment of women by providing a forum for exchange of ideas among our female authors.

With effort comes reward, but we will handle the stressful side of the business. We will help you gain the potential for a stream of income over time. Why would you limit yourself and the value of your work by accepting ghostwriting contracts to pay a small onetime fee? Freelancer has the word free in it because you are essentially selling your work at a minimal price compared to the value it can pos-sess. 

We look forward to helping you through the steps in the publishing process. We can help you publish your first, second, or subsequent books. We sign every Author to a one-book contract to start with, to allow Authors flexibility in their individual careers.  

What We Publish

Travel, Education, Poetry, Fantasy, Action, Crime, both Fiction and Non - Fiction. We specialize in shorter works. We are interested in everything & anything educational & factual. We do not support or publish material that is malicious or offensive in nature, or which involves personal attacks or hate speech. All submissions must comply with applicable laws.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create, keep and grow Author brands from around the world. We seek to publish all Authors who share our vision and meet our standards. We also provide a forum for women Authors from around the world allowing them to use our platform to share their message. We are committed to helping you achieve your own personal success and growth by developing and expanding your brand. We would like to take the blindfold off to allow you to see your full potential. Is your goal to get paid a flat fee to write books and short stories and blogs, or is it to have a potential stream of income from the works you have written?
